    Hammers Sitting Still

    This is my most recent Music and currently current project in Cambridge. An Acoustic duo with my wife, Kozbi, where we split singing and writing duties. Overtly spiritual and a chance for us to hone the huge inspiration we feel from quiet music to think about our changing selves. We recorded our first album "to Continue" in our apartment over snow storms together in Winter 2026 and it felt nice.
